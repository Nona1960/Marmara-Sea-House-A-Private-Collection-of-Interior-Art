Marmara Sea Apartment

The Historic Thermal Resort of Termal

One of the greatest advantages of the apartment is its proximity to the historic spa town of Termal, located just 30 minutes away by local minibus. The journey is simple and scenic, ending in one of Turkey’s most celebrated thermal parks.

Surrounded by centuries-old trees, the park is home to natural hot springs, traditional Turkish baths, thermal pools and elegant historic buildings. The springs have been renowned since Roman and Byzantine times, when the healing waters attracted noble families and Roman aristocracy seeking rest and recovery. In the early twentieth century, the resort was carefully restored under the patronage of Mustafa Kemal Atatürk, who frequently visited Termal and contributed to its development as one of Turkey’s leading health resorts.

The mineral-rich thermal waters are traditionally associated with relaxation and are widely used in spa therapy. They are particularly valued for bathing and wellness treatments aimed at supporting joint mobility, easing muscle tension and promoting general well-being. Fresh thermal mineral water can also be collected directly from the public springs, allowing visitors to continue enjoying it after returning home.

The rare combination of life by the Marmara Sea and immediate access to one of the Eastern Mediterranean’s historic thermal resorts gives this residence a unique character, where coastal living and centuries-old wellness traditions exist side by side.
