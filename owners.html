Marmara Sea Apartment

The Owners

We chose this apartment on the shores of the Marmara Sea for one reason above all others — its exceptional location. Reaching Yalova from Istanbul is effortless: a pleasant ferry journey across the sea brings you directly to the city, where the waterfront, markets and everyday life begin just a few steps from the terminal.

Everything that defines a refined Mediterranean lifestyle is within easy walking distance. The city’s renowned food market offers fresh vegetables, fruit, fish, cheeses and local delicacies every day. A long seaside promenade invites leisurely walks beneath the trees, while the elegant pedestrian streets are filled with cafés, bakeries and boutiques.

Only thirty minutes away lies the historic thermal resort of Termal, whose mineral springs have attracted visitors for centuries. A morning in the thermal park, lunch prepared from fresh local produce, an evening walk along the Marmara Sea and a stroll through Yalova’s lively shopping streets — this simple daily rhythm became the inspiration for choosing this home.

Over time, the apartment evolved into more than a seaside residence. It became a private artistic space where architecture, original paintings, travel and everyday life came together in harmony.
