Marmara Sea Apartment

Interior

The apartment occupies a privileged position on the first line of Yalova's waterfront park, overlooking the promenade that stretches along the shores of the Marmara Sea. Designed for both everyday comfort and contemplation, the residence combines generous natural light, uninterrupted sea views and a carefully curated artistic interior.

The layout includes a spacious living room with a panoramic window facing the Marmara Sea, an enclosed glass balcony, two bedrooms, a kitchen, a bathroom and an entrance hall. Every room is enriched with original paintings by contemporary artist Nona Dronova, creating an atmosphere where architecture and art form a single living environment.

Directly in front of the panoramic window stand two magnificent century-old plane trees whose broad crowns become a resting place for seabirds each evening. Throughout the day, the changing seascape offers an ever-evolving view of cargo ships, sailing yachts and passenger vessels travelling between the Bosphorus and the Dardanelles. After sunset, the distant lights of Istanbul and the Princes’ Islands appear on the horizon, completing one of the apartment’s most remarkable panoramic experiences.

The interior is conceived as a private gallery by the sea, where contemporary art, Mediterranean light and the rhythm of maritime life come together in quiet harmony.
