Marmara Sea Apartment

The greatest values of this apartment are its unique location and timeless connection with the sea. From the panoramic window opens a direct, breathtaking view of the Marmara Sea — a constantly changing natural painting of light, water and sky.

Another exceptional advantage is its proximity to the historic resort of Termal, famous for its thermal springs, where healing waters were appreciated since ancient times and visited by Roman aristocracy. This rare combination of sea, nature, history and culture creates a refined atmosphere of coastal living.
